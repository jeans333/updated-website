OmniFlow

App Design - Dec 2023

Overview

The ultimate multi-management application designed to revolutionize students' productivity and work-life balance. Omniflow features an interchangeable time manager, screen-time manager, schedule time-blocker, and more.

Tailored for students, it includes a unique study spot locator and a platform for networking with peers across campuses.
